# Gemini AI Product Photo Workflow — Reference

> Backup reference for the gold-store photography look. Feed this to Gemini with each product so it never forgets the target style.

## The Look (one-line summary)
Approachable luxury. Warm, organic, minimalist. Gold is the hero. Sells an everyday-elegance lifestyle, NOT stark traditional jewelry photography.

---

## 1. Overall Vibe & Style
- **Approachable luxury** — jewelry worn effortlessly every day, not locked in a vault.
- **Warm & organic** — natural, earthy, inviting (skin tones, fabrics).
- **Minimalist & uncluttered** — no distracting props, bold colors, or busy backgrounds. Gold is the undisputed hero.

## 2. Wardrobe & Styling
- **Fabric:** unbleached, textured linen or raw cotton. Matte rough texture contrasts smooth reflective gold.
- **Palette:** monochromatic warm — creamy off-whites, warm beiges, soft skin tones. Neutral canvas makes yellow gold pop without clashing.
- **Grooming:** naturally moisturized skin, soft glow, not oily/overdone. Sheer subtle-pink nail polish (no distraction).

## 3. Lighting Setup (the secret)
Gold behaves like a mirror — hardest part to get right.
- **Soft, diffused light** — no harsh shadows, no tiny blinding specular highlights. Never bare flash or direct sun.
- **Large light source, close to subject** — big softbox with diffusion, or large window with sheer white curtain.
- **Warm color temperature** — mimic golden-hour afternoon.
- **Reflectors (fill light)** — bounce white card opposite main light so shadows (under chin, between fingers) stay soft, not pitch black.
- **Manage reflections** — surround camera with white foam boards so gold reflects white space, not the dark studio/photographer. This keeps gold rich yellow.

## 4. Camera Composition & Settings
- **Shallow depth of field** — background + clothing behind product soft-blurred (bokeh). Low aperture f/2.8–f/4.
- **Tight framing** — cropped tight around product, little empty space.
- **Macro focus** — sharp on the leading edge of the jewelry; highlight stones, filigree, detail.

---

## Gemini Prompt Skeleton (paste + fill per product)
```
Generate a product photo of [PRODUCT: e.g. 21k yellow gold chain necklace].
Style: approachable luxury, warm and organic, minimalist. Gold is the hero.
Model wearing unbleached textured linen/raw cotton, warm beige palette, soft glowing skin.
Lighting: large soft diffused source (window + sheer curtain look), warm golden-hour temperature,
soft fill shadows, gold reflecting white space (rich yellow, no harsh specular highlights).
Camera: shallow depth of field f/2.8, tight crop, macro-sharp on jewelry detail, soft bokeh background.
Reference image attached: [PRODUCT-1 real photo].
```
